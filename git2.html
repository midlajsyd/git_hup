02 — Oceans

Oceans

The ocean covers more than 70% of the planet's surface. Its depths remain less explored than the surface of Mars — 95% of the ocean is still unseen by human eyes. It regulates our climate, absorbs our excess heat, and exhales half our oxygen.

Blue whales communicate through infrasound at frequencies so low they travel thousands of kilometres through open water — a language older than humanity itself.
